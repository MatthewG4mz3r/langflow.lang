ITALIAN | LESSON ONE

to say hello in italian you say ciao [ch-i-ow]


italian is very simple to learn if you learn it correctly.
for example, any word in english that ends in al is the same in
italian but ends in e [ei]
here is some examples

* legal - legale [le-gál-ei]
* global - globale [gloh-bál-ei]
* general - generale [jen-eh-rál-ei]

VOCAB FOR LESSON ONE

* legale [leh-gál-ei]
* illegale [il-eh-gál-ei]
* generale [jen-eh-rál-ei]
* locale [loh-cál-ei]
* ciao [ch-ee-ow]
